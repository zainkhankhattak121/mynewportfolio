About Me

Engineering AI
that matters.

I graduated in Software Engineering from COMSATS University Islamabad (2025) and have spent the last 3 years building AI systems that actually work in production — not just in theory. My work covers deep learning, NLP, voice AI, and full-stack development across mobile and web.

I have worked with clients across multiple countries on Fiverr as a Level 1 Seller, as well as on other freelance platforms and with local clients. My work ranges from AI chatbots and voice systems to React Native applications and FastAPI backends. I focus on writing clean, maintainable code and delivering solutions that genuinely solve problems, not just complete tasks.

For my Final Year Project, I built a deepfake audio detection system using WaveNet, Coqui TTS, gTTS, and Wav2Vec2, achieving 99.8% accuracy on real-world audio data. The system was designed for real deployment rather than just academic presentation, focusing on scalability and practical use.

I am also the Founder of Leeb AI, the VeriFy News App, and EYE-R. EYE-R is a dedicated assistive application designed to help color-blind individuals identify colors and perform color blindness testing.

In the VeriFy News App, I implemented a BERT-based real vs fake news detection model, combined with multiple verification APIs including Google Fact Check API, NewsAPI, GNews, and MediaStack to ensure reliable and cross-verified information.

In Leeb AI, I developed a multi-model AI platform integrating Gemini, Meta AI, ChatGPT, DeepSeek, and other leading models, allowing users to select their preferred model for chat or content generation. The platform also includes a one-click subscription and billing system for seamless user access.
