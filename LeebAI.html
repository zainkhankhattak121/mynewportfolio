LeebAI — AI-Powered Multi-Model Mobile App

During my internship at Devxcel, I built LeebAI — an innovative mobile platform that unifies GPT, Gemini, and Claude under one interface. I handled full frontend development and end-to-end functionality, delivering a clean, scalable AI chat experience with real-time messaging and voice support.

Key features & contributions

🤖 Multi‑Model AI Hub — Seamlessly switch between GPT, Gemini, and Claude within a single conversation thread.
🎙️ Voice-Powered Interaction — Integrated speech-to-text for hands-free messaging and AI commands.
⚡ Real‑time performance — Optimized WebSocket + FastAPI backend for low‑latency responses.
🔐 User Management — Secure authentication, conversation history, and personalized AI tools.
🧠 AI Productivity Tools — Summarization, code generation, and content rewriting inside the chat.

LeebAI showcases my expertise in React Native, modern LLM integrations, and crafting enterprise-ready AI applications. The project earned recognition at Devxcel for its clean architecture and delightful UX.
